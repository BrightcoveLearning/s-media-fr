Series availability details can be used to restrict access to a series based upon date, locations, devices and monetization plan. To view and edit the availability details for a series, open the Media module and click on a series name link. The series overview page will open. Click the Availability tab.

The availability settings are controlled using rule sets. All series must have at least one rule set. Rule sets are created to control access to a series based upon:

  • Start/end dates
  • Devices
  • Locations
  • Monetization
For information on creating and managing rule sets, see Creating and Managing Rule Sets.
