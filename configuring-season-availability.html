Season availability details can be used to restrict access to a season based upon date, locations, devices and monetization. To view and edit the availability details for a season, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Media module.
  2. Click All series in the panel navigation.
  3. Click on a series name link to open the series overview page.
  4. On the right side of the page, click on a season to open the season Overview page.
  5. Click the Availability tab.

The availability settings are controlled using rule sets. All seasons must have at least one rule set. Rule sets are created to control access to a season based upon:

  • Start/end dates
  • Devices
  • Locations
  • Monetization

For information on creating and managing rule sets, see Creating and Managing Rule Sets.
