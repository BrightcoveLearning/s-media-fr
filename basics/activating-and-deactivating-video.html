The Status column in the Media module indicates if the video is Activated or Deactivated.

  • - Indicates the video is active
  • - Indicates the video is inactive (can't be played)

If the Status column is not displayed, use the column selector to select it.

Steps

  1. To Activate a video, select it and then click More ˇ > Activate.
